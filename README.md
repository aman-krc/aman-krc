## 📊 Projects

### 🔵 [Sales & Revenue Analysis](https://github.com/aman-krc/superstore-sales-analysis)
> **SQL · Python · Pandas · Matplotlib**

Analysed 9,994 Superstore orders to identify revenue drivers, underperforming regions, and discount-profit relationships.
- Identified Central & South regions as 23% below avg revenue benchmark — traced to 2× higher discount rates
- Found that orders with 30%+ discounts have a **100% loss rate** — every single order loses money
- Built 6-chart visual dashboard covering monthly trends, top customers, category margins, and regional performance

---

### 🟣 [Customer Churn Analysis](https://github.com/aman-krc/customer-churn-analysis)
> **Python · Pandas · SQL · EDA**

Analysed telecom customer data to identify key churn drivers and high-risk customer segments.
- Performed EDA on tenure, contract type, payment method, and monthly charges
- Identified high-risk segments and provided data-backed retention recommendations
- Used SQL queries and Python visualisations to communicate findings

---

### 🟡 [Power BI Performance Dashboard](https://github.com/aman-krc/powerbi-performance-dashboard)
> **Power BI · DAX · Power Query**

Built an interactive executive dashboard tracking revenue, profit, and growth KPIs.
- Wrote DAX measures for YoY growth, profit margin %, and Sales YTD
- Designed drill-through pages and dynamic slicers for Region, Category, and Date Range
- Used star schema data model with fact and dimension tables

---

### 🟢 [Excel KPI & Reporting Project](https://github.com/aman-krc/excel-kpi-reporting)
> **Excel · Pivot Tables · Power Query**

Created a full KPI tracking system with automated summaries and executive-level reporting.
- Built dynamic Pivot Tables for monthly, regional, and category performance
- Automated summaries using XLOOKUP, SUMIF, COUNTIF, and nested IF formulas
- Designed an interactive dashboard with slicers and conditional formatting
